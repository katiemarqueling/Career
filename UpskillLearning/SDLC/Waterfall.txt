Waterfall Notes
 
 - Linear, each phase  must be completed before moving on to the next. 
 - Everything is planned on the front end. Must really understand what the customer is looking for. 

 - 5 Phases
    - Requirement
      - What customer wants
         - Assumptions
         - Dependencies
         - Risk
         - Cost 
         - Time
         - Success Statistics
    - Design
      - What is the technical solution
         - Projected Specs
         - Models
         - Hardware and software
    - Implementation
      -Develop Code from Design
    - Verification
      - Customer Approves
    - Maintenance
      - Fix Bugs Overtime
      - Updated Versions
